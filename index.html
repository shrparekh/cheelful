WELCOME

WHERE PLAY IS PURPOSEFUL AND LEARNING IS PLAYFUL

Our philosophy revolves around the idea that children learn best through play. Play is not just a way for children to pass the time; it is their way of understanding the world, experimenting with ideas, and developing crucial skills.

INQUIRY-BASED
LEARNING

Our curriculum is built around inquiry, encouraging children to ask questions, investigate, and make discoveries through hands-on experiences.

PLAY-BASED
APPROACH

We believe in the power of play as a natural and effective way for children to learn, develop social skills, and express their creativity.

CULTURAL
DIVERSITY

We celebrate diversity and promote cultural understanding, preparing children to be global citizens with respect for different perspectives and backgrounds.

1:5 STUDENT
TEACHER RATIO

Our small class sizes ensure personalised attention, meaningful interactions, and individualised support for every child's learning journey.

ECOLOGICAL
LEARNING

We instill a sense of environmental responsibility through nature-based learning experiences, gardening activities, and lessons on conservation, nurturing a deep connection with the natural world.

HOLISTIC
EDUCATION

We nurture the physical, emotional, social, and cognitive development of each child, providing a well-rounded education that prepares them for future challenges and opportunities.

SOCIAL-EMOTIONAL
LEARNING

Our curriculum emphasises social-emotional skills such as empathy, cooperation, resilience, fostering positive relationships, emotional well-being, and effective communication among students.

PARENT
PARTNERSHIP

We believe fostering partnerships with parents, involving child's learning. Through collaboration, shared decision-making, create supportive community.

Our Philosophy

Nurturing a Vibrant
Community

We instill in each child a love for learning, curiosity, and a foundation for lifelong success. Our unique blend of the play way method and Montessori approach creates a joyful, engaging, and meaningful learning environment where children thrive, explore, and develop a lifelong love for learning.

Our Programs
FAQ
Can I walk in to visit the school for admission?
We request parents to call and inquire before visiting. This helps us ensure a staff member is available to assist you personally.
FAQ
Do you offer school visits or trial classes before admission?
Yes, school visits are available from Monday to Friday between 2:00 PM and 4:00 PM (non-school hours). However, we do not offer free trial classes.
FAQ
When are admissions open?
Admissions are open throughout the year, subject to seat availability. We recommend early inquiry for preferred batches.
FAQ
What are the school timings?
The school operates from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Timings vary depending on the child’s class and batch and will be shared during admission.
FAQ
Do you take mid-year admissions?
Yes, we follow rolling admissions depending on seat availability.
FAQ
Are you open on public holidays?
No, the school remains closed on most public holidays.
FAQ
What curriculum do you follow?
We follow a thoughtfully blended curriculum combining play-way and Montessori methods. Our programs are designed to foster creativity, independence, and a smooth transition into formal schooling.
FAQ
How do you ensure children are school-ready by Kindergarten?
We focus on developing a strong foundation through phonics, early numeracy, social skills, and hands-on exploration. Our curriculum gradually introduces routines, structured learning, and self-management—all of which prepare children confidently for primary school environments.
FAQ
What is the teacher-to-child ratio?
Our teacher-to-child ratio is 1:6, ensuring personalized attention and care for every child.
FAQ
Are your staff trained in early childhood care and safety?
Yes, all our teachers are trained in early childhood education and first aid protocols.
FAQ
Do you provide snacks or meals?
Children bring their own food from home. We provide a recommended snack list to ensure consistency and nutritional balance.
FAQ
Do you offer transport facilities?
No, we currently do not offer transport facilities. Parents are requested to make their own pick-up and drop arrangements.
FAQ
Do you offer daycare or after-school care?
Yes, we offer extended daycare hours and engaging after-school programs for children who stay beyond regular school time.
